Lease-Up Scheduling & Preparation

  1. Inspections will confirm via email with the intake coordinator when the unit passes inspection and the final rent amount is approved. The client or service provider must be contacted within 24 hours to schedule the appointment.
  2. Request updated income information from the client as well as all adult members consistent with DHCD policies. Inform the client that all adult members must be present at the appointment and bring photo IDs to check in with the front desk.
  3. Once the appointment has been scheduled, run action "6.5000 Lease-Up Scheduled". The effective date is the date the appointment was confirmed. Update the tracking sheet and leave a priority 1 note.
  1. Click on the "Images" tile and download the "Rent Reasonable" sheet. Print this out and place it in the client's file.
  2. Create the lease filling in the relevant information based on the printed RFTA for the unit information. The final rent amount noted on the rent reasonable sheet will need to be filled in under the "Rent" field of the lease. The "Rent Share" and "Lease effective dates" field will be filled in by the staff person conducting the lease-up appointment.
  3. Create the HAP contract similarly to the lease. The "Family Composition" field must include all members that are noted in the printed RFTA. The "PHA Portion" field will be filled in by the staff person conducting the lease-up appointment.
  4. The property owner courtesy letter and VAWA notice must also included.
