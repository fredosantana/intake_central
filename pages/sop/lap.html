Language Assistance Protocol

The Language Assistance Protocol is aimed to provide clients who disclose that their primary language is not English with interpretation and translation services.

  1. Cross reference the client's Communication Preference Form to ensure if a client requires interpretation/translation services. Update Tracker entry if necessary under the client's "Member" tile.
  2. Fill out the LAP Form with the client's information, language preference requested, and details of the appointment. If the request is for an issuance briefing, two requests must be created. One for the first hour and another for the second.
  3. Print the request, drop it off in the Section 8 LAP contact person's inbox, and email the contact person as well as cc the intake coordinator to confirm the request was made.
  4. The LAP contact person will coordinate with volunteer staff persons to arrange for interpretation/translation services. Follow-up appropriately once this has been taken care of.
