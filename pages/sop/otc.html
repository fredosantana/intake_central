On The Clock

After a client has been deemed eligible for the program and has been issued a voucher via an issuance briefing, the following steps must be performed in Tracker immediately.

  1. Ensure family composition is accurate by checking the "Member" tile. Update information if necessary.
  2. Update income information in the "Income" tile consistent with the income that was intially reported.
  3. Click "Perform Action", then click on the appropriate program allocation. The action ID will be "6.000 On The Clock". The effective date will be the date the voucher was issued.
  4. Check the OTC tile to ensure the clock is on.
  5. Click "Calc". Select "10 - Issuance of Voucher". The effective date will be the date the voucher was issued.
  6. After running calc, print the first page of the 50058. Leave the print out in the file.
  7. Update the tracking sheet to reflect clock dates and file location.
